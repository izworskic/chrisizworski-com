Chris Izworski, Prepared and 911 Technology

Solutions Consultant · Prepared · Emergency Communications

Chris Izworski is a Solutions Consultant at Prepared, a public safety technology company that deploys audio capture and analysis systems at 911 centers across the country. He came to the role from a dozen years of 911 director leadership in Michigan: Bay County 911 from 2013 to 2022, Saginaw County 911 from 2022 to 2025. That operational background is the foundation for technology deployment work that happens inside live dispatch environments.

Prepared's systems integrate with the call handling equipment already inside dispatch centers, the hardware and software stacks from Motorola, Intrado, Solacom, and other vendors that receive and process every 911 call. Deploying successfully in that environment requires knowing how those systems work together, what the center's constraints are, and how to coordinate with dispatch leadership, IT staff, and vendors simultaneously. Chris Izworski's role is to make that happen at each deployment site.

The Saginaw County AI Deployment

Before joining Prepared, Chris Izworski led the deployment of one of Michigan's first AI-powered non-emergency call systems at Saginaw County 911 in 2024. That project, covered by NENA, WNEM TV5, WCMU Public Radio, and Fast Company, is the most visible example of his direct AI implementation experience in a 911 environment.
