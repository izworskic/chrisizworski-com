Tools

Calculators, references, and infrastructure built from direct operational and gardening work.

These tools started as things built to solve specific problems, planting timing questions that kept coming up at Freighter View Farms, the need for a reliable daily summary of Great Lakes vessel activity, a reusable repository for the AI agent instruction work that came out of the Saginaw County 911 deployment. They are practical tools, not marketing assets. Each one is linked to the context that produced it.

Gardening Tools, Zone 6a Michigan

Zone 6a Planting Calculator, Bay City and Saginaw Bay
Month-by-month seed starting and transplant guide calibrated to the Bay City frost window. Covers tomatoes, peppers, brassicas, cucurbits, and direct-sow crops. Built from years of season tracking at Freighter View Farms. The most precise Zone 6a timing resource for the Great Lakes Bay Region.
Heirloom Variety Matchmaker
Five questions about your garden, three heirloom varieties matched to it, each with seed saving instructions. Covers zone, sun exposure, growing space, experience level, and what you want from the harvest. Built on the Freighter View Farms variety trials.
Heirloom Seed Saving Guide
Fermentation method for tomatoes, dry processing for beans and squash, isolation distances, harvest timing, and storage conditions for Zone 6a Michigan. Written from the Freighter View Farms seed library. Specific enough to use directly.
Michigan Frost Dates, Great Lakes Bay Region
Last spring frost and first fall frost data for Michigan growing regions, with specific context for the lake-effect zone around Bay City and Saginaw Bay. Covers how proximity to the lake moderates frost timing compared to inland locations at the same latitude.
Seed Starting Guide
Equipment, medium, lighting, and timing for indoor seed starts in Zone 6a Michigan. Covers the setup that actually works for a home gardener without a greenhouse, not the ideal setup, the practical one.

Great Lakes and Maritime Tools

Great Lakes Gazette, Daily Maritime Brief
A daily newsletter updated automatically each morning with vessel movements, port reports, water levels across all five Great Lakes, and National Weather Service marine forecasts. Built on live AIS vessel tracking data and NOAA feeds. Free and updated every day during the navigation season.
Great Lakes Freighter Tracking Guide
How to watch vessel traffic on the Great Lakes using live AIS data, which tools to use, how to read vessel information, and what the seasonal patterns look like from Bay City and the Soo Locks.
SS Edmund Fitzgerald, November 10 1975, The Wreck on Lake Superior
The complete history of the Edmund Fitzgerald, the largest freighter ever lost on the Great Lakes. The ship, the final voyage, the 29-man crew with hometowns and roles, the wreck site 17 miles from Whitefish Point, the July 4 1995 bell recovery, the Gordon Lightfoot song, the annual memorial ceremonies, and how to visit Whitefish Point today.
Soo Locks Live Vessel Activity and Visitor Guide
Interactive live AIS vessel map at the Soo Locks in Sault Ste. Marie, Michigan. Real-time navigation season status, lock dimensions and history, the Poe replacement lock under construction, vessel category identification, and the visitor guide for watching transits at the busiest commercial lock complex in the world.
Northern Lights Michigan, Tonight's Aurora Forecast and Viewing Guide
Live NOAA SWPC three-day Kp index forecast for Michigan, tonight's viewing odds calculated from peak Kp, the dark-sky locations on Lake Superior and the Upper Peninsula that can actually see the aurora when it shows up, dark-sky park ratings, photography settings, and the geomagnetic storm science.
Great Lakes Buoy Dashboard, Live Wave, Wind, and Water Conditions
Live data from more than 100 NOAA buoys and coastal stations across all five Great Lakes. Wave height, wind speed and gusts, water temperature, and air pressure on an interactive dark-themed map with activity filters for fishing, kayaking, swimming, sailing, and diving. Geolocation-based distance ranking, 5-day sparkline history per station, and conditional cross-promotion to the rest of the Great Lakes tool network.

Michigan Rivers and Outdoors

Michigan Fall Color, a Live Peak Map, Forecast, and Rivers to Paddle Through the Color
Where Michigan's fall color is peaking right now, from the Upper Peninsula to the southern border, built on live NASA satellite canopy readings and Open-Meteo weather. Region-by-region peak timing on an interactive dark map, a six-day forecast for each area, a color-near-you locator, a trip planner that matches a weekend and a mood to where the color is standing, eleven destination field guides from the Tunnel of Trees to the Au Sable, and a short note each day through the season. Free and updated daily.
The Au Sable Field Map, 70 Verified Access Points, Campgrounds, and a Trip Planner
An interactive map of Michigan's Au Sable River from Grayling to Lake Huron, with 70 verified locations: launches and liveries, fly shops and lodges, state and federal campgrounds including the impoundment camps, scenic overlooks, hiking and Shore-to-Shore horseback trails, and birding hotspots from loons to the Kirtland's warbler. Every card carries live USGS flow and weather, there is a float-time planner for the mainstem and its branches, activity filters for fishing, paddling, camping, birding and more, and notes on the steelhead and salmon water below Foote Dam.
Au Sable River Guide, Float Times, Fishing, and Camping
A planning guide to the Au Sable from Grayling to Lake Huron: the Holy Water and the river's segments, popular floats with paddle times, where to fish for trout, smallmouth, and the steelhead and salmon run below Foote, the state, federal, and impoundment campgrounds, birding from loons to the Kirtland's warbler, and answers to the questions people ask most.
Michigan Outdoors Now, Live-Condition Day Trip Planner
Start with a Michigan city or ZIP and get three outdoor day-trip plans ranked for forecast, air quality, drive time, interests, kids, dogs, and accessibility. Covers 27 curated destinations, uses live weather signals, requires no login, and does not save your location. Built by Chris Izworski. Free.

Trip Planners

Traverse City Wine Country, a Routed Day Planner for the Wineries, Breweries, Cideries, and Distilleries
A day planner for the tasting rooms of the Traverse City area, across the Old Mission and Leelanau peninsulas. Choose the places you want to visit and it builds a routed, time-aware loop that respects real opening hours, the time you spend at each stop, and the drive between them, with beaches, hikes, and scenic overlooks woven in along the way. Sample starter days to begin from, a designated-driver mode, a shareable summary you can drop in a text, and a button that opens the whole loop in Google Maps. Built on a verified dataset of 74 venues: 40 wineries, 22 breweries, 7 distilleries, and 5 cideries. Free.
Plan by area or trip: Old Mission wineries · Leelanau wine trail · Suttons Bay tasting rooms · breweries and distilleries · one-day itineraries · group and bachelorette planning · fall color wine tour
Pictured Rocks Planner, Boat, Kayak, Hike, or Drive
A logistics-first planner for Pictured Rocks National Lakeshore. An interactive decision tool weighs your time, walking tolerance, water comfort, and who is coming, then hands you the right day: the boat cruise, a guided sea kayak trip, the 10-mile Chapel Loop, or the drive-and-stroll circuit. Covers the honest comparison between all four, the Munising versus Grand Marais lodging fork, reservation-only camping and the six-month backcountry permit window, entrance fees, where dogs can and cannot go, the south-wind biting fly problem, month-by-month timing, and current closures including the Munising Falls rebuild. Free.
Lake Superior Circle Tour, Stop-by-Stop Guide and Trip Planner
Complete guide to the 1,300-mile circumnavigation of Lake Superior. 31 major stops across Michigan, Wisconsin, Minnesota, and Ontario; freighter-watching notes at every port; three concrete itineraries (7, 10, and 14 days); live water-level data; and an interactive trip planner. Built from years of Great Lakes work, wilderness canoe trips on the Canadian north shore, and the freighter routes that pass Bay City on the way to the Soo Locks.

AI and Agent Infrastructure

AgentBase, Open Agent Instruction Registry
An open registry of agent instruction files, system prompts, skills, workflows, domain packs, and safety filters for AI agents. CC0-licensed and MCP-compatible. Built from the implementation work that produced the Saginaw County 911 AI deployment and the Prepared deployment tooling. Free to use, fork, or extend.
