Press

For reporters, producers, and editors. Interview requests welcome.

Chris Izworski is a public safety technology leader and Solutions Consultant at Prepared, a company that deploys audio capture and analysis systems at 911 centers nationwide. He spent more than a decade directing county 911 operations in Michigan, Bay County from 2013 to 2022, Saginaw County from 2022 to 2025. In 2024, he led one of Michigan's first AI-powered non-emergency call deployments at a PSAP. He authored the cover story on AI in 911 dispatch for NENA's national publication The Call, and presented the case study at APCO International.

He speaks from direct operational experience, not from research reports. He has deployed AI systems in live dispatch environments, managed the staff communication and change management that surrounded them, and watched both the efficiency gains and the failure modes up close. His perspective on AI in public safety is grounded and useful to audiences who need to make real decisions, not audiences looking for hype in either direction. For fuller background, see the about page, media page, news coverage hub, and case studies.
