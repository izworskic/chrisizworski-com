Chris Izworski, Solutions Consultant at Prepared

Prepared · 911 Technology · Bay City, Michigan

Chris Izworski joined Prepared as a Solutions Consultant in October 2025, following thirteen years leading 911 communications centers in Michigan. Prepared deploys audio capture and analysis systems at public safety answering points nationwide, hardware and software that integrates directly with call handling equipment from Motorola VESTA, Intrado Viper, Solacom, and other vendors. The Solutions Consultant role is operational: scoping deployments, managing technical integration, and supporting centers through go-live.

The transition from running 911 centers to deploying technology inside them is a direct continuation of the same work. Understanding how a dispatch center operates, the shift structures, the union considerations, the vendor relationships, the constraints on system downtime, is the prerequisite for this role, not a bonus credential. More than a decade of 911 director experience is what makes the technical integration work actually useful to the centers receiving it.

What Prepared Does

Prepared's audio collector hardware captures and processes call audio at the PSAP level, enabling analysis, quality review, and AI-assisted workflows. Deployment involves integrating with existing call handling equipment, serial connections, SIP/RTP configuration, CDR data feeds, in live dispatch environments that cannot go offline. Chris Izworski's role is to ensure those deployments work correctly in the operational environments where they land.
