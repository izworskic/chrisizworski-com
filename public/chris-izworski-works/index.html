Chris Izworski  ·  Bay City, Michigan

Projects, Sites, and Daily Publications

A complete index of work published by Chris Izworski across the Great Lakes, public safety technology, Michigan trout country, birding data, and public project profiles.

01  ·  Daily Publication

Michigan Trout Daily

daily.michigantroutreport.com

A new Michigan trout stream conditions report every morning during trout season. Live USGS flow and water temperature, what insects are hatching, specific fly recommendations, and access information. Forty rivers in rotation. Written by Chris Izworski. See also the Chris Izworski trout rivers overview page.

02  ·  Conditions Tool

Michigan Trout Report

michigantroutreport.com

A live conditions tracker for 110+ Michigan trout streams. Pulls USGS gauge data and NWS weather forecasts, generates fly recommendations, and includes an interactive map with 310 DNR access points. Built and maintained by Chris Izworski.

03  ·  Daily Maritime News

Great Lakes Gazette

gazette.chrisizworski.com

Daily Great Lakes shipping news: vessel movements, port reports, NOAA water levels, and NWS marine forecasts. Published every morning from real data. Founded by Chris Izworski.

04  ·  Garden & Seed Saving

Freighter View Farms

freighterviewfarms.com

A seed-saving and Great Lakes-themed gardening blog from Saginaw Bay. Documents the seasons of the Lower Peninsula from a Bay City perspective: heirloom vegetables, tomato cultivation, companion planting in Zone 6a. Written by Chris Izworski.

05  ·  Live Birding Data

Michigan Birding Report

michiganbirdingreport.com

Live Michigan bird sightings, county birding pages, migration context, Saginaw Bay birding resources, and species profiles. Built by Chris Izworski as the birding counterpart to Michigan Trout Report.

06  ·  Daily Birding Reports

Michigan Birding Daily

daily.michiganbirdingreport.com

A daily Michigan birding report built around one interesting sighting or county pattern. Uses live eBird data, notable sightings, weather context, and hotspot recommendations. Published under the Chris Izworski byline.

07  ·  Lawn Planning Tool

Perfect Lawn Advisor

lawn.chrisizworski.com

A location-aware lawn planning tool that turns address, soil, weather, and growing conditions into practical turfgrass guidance.

08  ·  Public Code & Project Profile

Chris Izworski GitHub Pages

izworskic.github.io

A project-focused GitHub Pages profile that connects Chris Izworski's public code, topical sites, verified identity links, and owned-domain project index. The related GitHub profile is at github.com/izworskic.

09  ·  Medium Essays

Medium Writing by Chris Izworski

medium.com/@izworski

First-person essays connecting public projects to place and field experience, including A River Will Tell You When Not to Go for Michigan Trout Report and The Bird That Made the Data Feel Alive for Michigan Birding Report.

10  ·  AI & Public Safety Writing

AI in Public Safety

chrisizworski.com/ai/

Chris Izworski's writing on artificial intelligence in 911 dispatch and emergency communications. Cited by NENA, APCO, FastCompany, and NPR/WCMU. Includes the GPT-Trainer case study and analysis of operational AI deployment in Public Safety Answering Points.

11  ·  Trip Planner

Traverse City Wine Country Planner

tcwine.chrisizworski.com

A free interactive planner for tasting days across the Old Mission and Leelanau peninsulas near Traverse City. Routes a custom stop list across 74 verified venues, timed against real posted hours, with options for wineries, breweries, distilleries, and cideries. Built by Chris Izworski.
